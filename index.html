Conspiracy LARP

A distributed, permissionless alternate reality game of diegetic conspiracy satire.

Conspiracy theory hijacks real questions and answers them with a lie so lurid the real question can never be asked again. We think that theft is worth fighting, and that the weapon is comedy rather than correction.

So people build things. Fake intranets, fake filings, character blogs, departments that never existed — artifacts you are meant to encounter as real, salted with jokes for whoever looks closely. Anyone can join and nobody is in charge.

The Player & Creator's Kit The manual: what this is, how to build a corner of it, and the ethics we hold ourselves to. Start here.
